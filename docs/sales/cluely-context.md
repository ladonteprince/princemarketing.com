## CLOSING PATTERNS

### Trial close (use mid-call)
*"If we hit those metrics in 60 days, what does the next phase look like for you?"*

### Soft close
*"Where would you want to start — the 60-day Pilot to de-risk it, or jump into Series since you already have the budget?"*

### Hard close
*"I have one Pilot slot opening this month and one in [next month]. Which one do you want?"*

### Stall handler ("send me a proposal")
*"Happy to. Before I do — is there a number above which this becomes a 'no' regardless of what I write? I'd rather know now and design the proposal to fit, than send something off."*

### Stall handler ("I need to talk to my partner")
*"Totally — who else makes this call with you? Let's get them on a 15-min follow-up so they're not deciding from a forwarded email."*

## WHAT NEVER TO SAY (Cluely: suppress these patterns)

- ❌ "Hopefully…" / "Maybe we could…" / "I think…" — premium clients pay for certainty
- ❌ Listing features. List outcomes.
- ❌ "Our package includes 16 videos" → ✅ "You'll have 16 episodes live by day 30."
- ❌ Apologizing for price.
- ❌ "Let me check with my team" if you don't have a team — say "let me confirm by EOD" instead.
- ❌ Discounting before a commitment. Trade, don't give.
